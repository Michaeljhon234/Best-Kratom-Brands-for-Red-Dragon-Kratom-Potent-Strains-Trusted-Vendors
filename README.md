### **Can Red Dragon kratom be used during daytime hours?**

At very low doses under 2 grams, some users report mild stimulating effects from Red Dragon. However, its dominant alkaloid character trends sedating at moderate doses. ### **What storage conditions preserve Red Dragon alkaloid potency?**

Store Red Dragon powder in an airtight, opaque container. Keep it away from heat sources, direct light, and humidity. Oxygen exposure accelerates alkaloid oxidation and degrades potency within months. Properly stored powder retains full potency for 12 to 24 months. Refrigeration is unnecessary if the container maintains a complete seal between uses.
